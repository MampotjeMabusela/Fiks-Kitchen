OUR STORY

Cooking Is Our Love Language

Fiks Kitchen was born from a deep love of African food culture — the umuntu spirit of feeding people well, feeding people together. We don't just cater events. We create memories on plates. Every dish carries the warmth of home, the boldness of tradition, and the finesse of a professional kitchen. That's the Fiks Touch.

SIGNATURE MENU

Flavours That Tell Our Story

Bold, authentic African dishes crafted with love and the finest ingredients.

Signature

Slow-cooked Oxtail Stew

Tender oxtail braised for hours in a rich, spiced gravy with root vegetables. Served with pap and gravy — a true South African classic.

Umleqwa (Free-range Chicken)

Traditional spiced village chicken, slow-cooked until fall-off-the-bone tender. Seasoned with African herbs and served with its own rich broth.

Signature

Braai Lamb Chops

Thick-cut lamb chops marinated in our signature African herb rub, flame-grilled to perfection with a smoky char and juicy centre.

Beef Mogodu

Tripe slow-cooked to perfection in a savoury stew with onions, peppers, and traditional spices. A beloved township delicacy done right.

Peri-Peri Whole Grilled Chicken

Whole chicken marinated in fiery peri-peri sauce and grilled over open flame. Crispy skin, juicy meat, and a kick that keeps you coming back.

Bobotie with Yellow Rice

The Cape Malay classic — spiced minced meat baked with an egg topping, served alongside fragrant yellow rice with raisins and turmeric.

Signature

Chakalaka & Pap

The OG combo — spicy vegetable relish loaded with carrots, beans, and peppers, paired with smooth maize pap. The heartbeat of every feast.

Umngqusho (Samp & Beans)

Hearty samp and sugar beans simmered together until creamy and comforting. A Xhosa staple that nourishes body and soul.

Spinach & Groundnut Stew (Morogo)

Wild spinach wilted in a creamy groundnut sauce with onions and tomatoes. Earthy, rich, and deeply African.

Roasted Sweet Potato with Cinnamon Butter

Caramelised sweet potato wedges roasted until golden, finished with a swirl of cinnamon-infused butter. Sweet, warm, irresistible.

Dombolo (Steamed Bread)

Fluffy steamed bread dumplings that soak up every drop of gravy. Soft, pillowy, and the perfect companion to any stew.

Fried Plantain

Ripe plantain sliced and fried until caramelised on the edges. A sweet, golden side that bridges West and Southern African flavours.

Signature

Malva Pudding with Custard

Warm, sticky sponge pudding drenched in buttery caramel sauce, served with velvety custard. The dessert that ends every great South African meal.

Koeksisters

Twisted, syrup-drenched Afrikaner pastries — crispy on the outside, sticky-sweet on the inside. Pure indulgence in every bite.

Peppermint Crisp Tart

Layers of Tennis biscuits, caramel treat, and whipped cream studded with Peppermint Crisp shards. A South African braai legend.

Signature

Umqombothi Caramel Cake

Our fusion original — traditional sorghum beer notes infused into a moist caramel layer cake. Bold, unique, unmistakably Fiks.

Fried Fat Cakes (Magwinya) with Jam

Golden, puffy vetkoek fried fresh and served with sweet strawberry jam. A township favourite that never goes out of style.

Homemade Hibiscus (Zobo) Juice

Deep crimson hibiscus brew sweetened with ginger and pineapple. Refreshing, antioxidant-rich, and beautifully African.

Ginger Beer (Traditional)

Fiery homemade ginger beer brewed the old-fashioned way. Fizzy, spicy, and the perfect palate cleanser between courses.

Rooibos Iced Tea

South Africa's native rooibos steeped and chilled, served over ice with a sprig of mint. Naturally caffeine-free and elegantly refreshing.

Fruit Punch with Mint

A vibrant blend of tropical fruits, citrus, and fresh mint. Colourful, celebratory, and perfect for any event toast.
